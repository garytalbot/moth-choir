Ritual Tide

Click or touch to set beacons. Particles bend toward the nearest beacons, then drift away when the tide grows loud. The motion is deterministic by seed, and you can export the current moment when the tide looks right.

Controls: Space pause, R reseed, C clear, B add random beacon, P pulse toggle, S save.
