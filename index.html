A small browser creature page. No spreadsheets. No dashboards. Just warm light, silk-thin wings, and a room that keeps trying to sing itself into shape.

Click anywhere to place a lamp. Move the mouse to become the brightest thing in the room. Click or tap Wake hum to start the ambient layer. Hold Conduct and drag to draw a temporary light-score the moths will follow. Keys: D dim, S scatter, L lamp, M moon, C conduct, H hum, P postcard, R reset. The postcard exports the current night as an SVG and can jump into the share sheet when the browser allows it.
